5."We must be free or die, who speak the tongue that
Shakespeare spake; the faith and morals hold Which
Milton held." This quote is from:

A. Lines Written in Early Spring

B, The Excursion

C. It is Not to be Thought of That the Flood

D. My Heart Leaps up When I Behold

Answer: C. It is Not to be Thought of That the Flood

6. In which poem does the quote "I travelled among
unknown men, In lands beyond the sea; Not England!
did I know till then What love I bore to thee" appear?

A. Nutting

B. Lines Composed of Few Miles Above Tintern
Abbey

C. I Travelled Among Unknown Men

D. The Excursion

Answer: C. I Travelled Among Unknown Men
