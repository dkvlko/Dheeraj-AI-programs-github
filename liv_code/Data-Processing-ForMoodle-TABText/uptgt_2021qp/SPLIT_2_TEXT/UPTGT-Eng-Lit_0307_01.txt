Quotations from "Paradise Lost ”

1. In which part of "Paradise Lost" is the quote "Rhyme
being lame meter, but the invention of a barbarous
age, to set off wretched matter" found?

a) Book 1

b) Book 2

c) "The Verse" (preface added 1668)

d) Book 4

Answer: c) "The Verse" (preface added 1668)

2. Who is referred to as "The infernal serpent" in
"Paradise Lost"?

a) Beelzebub

b) Belial

c) Satan

d) Moloch

Answer: c) Satan
