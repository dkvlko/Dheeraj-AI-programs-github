3. What characteristic did John Aubrey note about
Milton's pronunciation of the letter "R"?

a) It was soft and gentle

b) It was loud and clear

c) It was very hard, a sign of a satirical wit

d) It was barely noticeable

Answer: c) It was very hard, a sign of a satirical wit

4. According to William Blake, why did Milton write

b) Because he was a true poet and of the devil's
party without knowing it

c) Because he found them more interesting

d) Because he feared writing about Angels and God

devil's party without knowing it
