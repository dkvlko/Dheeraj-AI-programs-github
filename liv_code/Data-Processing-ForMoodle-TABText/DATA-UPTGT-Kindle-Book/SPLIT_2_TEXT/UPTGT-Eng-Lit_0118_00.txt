19. In "Antony and Cleopatra," why is Cleopatra upset
with Antony in Act 1, Scene 3?

A) He is leaving her for Rome

B) He has married Octavia

C) He refuses to fight

D) He betrayed her to Caesar

Answer: A) He is leaving her for Rome

20. In "Macbeth," what is Lady Macbeth's role in
Duncan's murder?

A) She actually kills Duncan

B) She orchestrates the murder and convinces
Macbeth to do it

C) She provides an alibi for Macbeth

D) She warns Duncan of the plot

Answer: B) She orchestrates the murder and

convinces Macbeth to do it
