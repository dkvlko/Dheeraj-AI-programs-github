1. Who wrote: "The Mind of Man - My haunt, and the
main region of my song"?

A. Samuel Taylor Coleridge

B. John Keats

C. William Wordsworth

D. Percy Bysshe Shelley

Answer: C. William Wordsworth

2. In which book does the quote "The good die first,
And they whose hearts are dry as summer dust Burn
to the socket" appear?

A. The Excursion

B, The Prelude

C. Lyrical Ballads

D. Peter Bell

Answer: A. The Excursion
