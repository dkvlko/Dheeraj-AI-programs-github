1. Which of the following works is not written by William Wordsworth?
a) The Thorn
b) The Idiot Boy
c) Simon Lee
d) Beyond
Answer: Beyond
2. Who refers to William Wordsworth as an "ever enduring man"?
a) Dr. Johnson
b) John Keats
c) De Quincey
d) S.T. Coleridge
Answer: S.T. Coleridge
3. A Modern Comedy is written by:
a) John Galsworthy
b) William Shakespeare
c) Ben Jonson
d) Sheridan
Answer: John Galsworthy
4. John Galsworthy's ideas on drama are found in:
a) The Inn of Tranquillity
b) The Patrician
c) Maid in waiting
d) Indian Summer of a Forsyte
Answer: The Inn of Tranquillity
5. Cokeson is a character in:
a) Justice
b) Macbeth
c) Areopagitica
d) As You Like it
Answer: Justice
